Groups

It is often necessary to distinguish singular ideas into larger groups. Buy Avocados given the proper context probably lives inside a shopping list, inside a memo pad, perhaps on a magnet on a refrigerator, in our apartment. We group elements to give them context in order to add semantic meaning. Properly grouped elements also make application of stylesheets more elegant.

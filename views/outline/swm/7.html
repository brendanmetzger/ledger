Read program structure from Eloquent JavasScript.

Here we are standing at the midpoint of the semester; a design hurdle should have been cleared, your efforts should be paying off at this point*. I want you to reread Chapter 3 of the css manual and this time around everything should be relatively understood. At this point you can finish up Chapters 4 and 5 that cover inheritance and specificity, among other topics. From here on we embark only moving forward into application, which is to say, our stylesheets should be evolving, becoming more advanced and thinking more and more about context and interactivity.

*I say should be paying off in terms of rapidly advancing techniques and skills. If the material in Chapter 3 sounds unfamiliar or you come away thinking when would I ever use this? then you are probably going to have give considerably more effort in the second half of this course.

Presentations

Project Presentations need not take more than five minutes. Part of the goal is to sell an idea and you should project confidence with that idea and the effort you have made.

For the Presenter

  • Be ready with your work and ready to talk about it.
  • Highlight any obstacles or difficult parts you were able to solve.
  • Invite constructive criticism and ask for feedback.

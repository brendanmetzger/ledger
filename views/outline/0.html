Workflow

Today's lecture is titled mis en place, a French culinary phrase which means everything in its place, because it is the one thing every student can easily achieve that will guarantee a positive impact in any profession involving precision and craft. Having the proper resources and tools (and correct usage) will both reduce the amount of mistakes and increase the amount of joy you feel with your efferts.

  • A text editor
  • A modern web browser (Google's Chrome will be used in class and te evaluate all coursework)
  • An FTP Client
  • The Web Development console (built into modern browsers)
  • The W3C Validators (Markup and Cascading Style Sheets)
  • Awareness of documentation and resources (Such as The Mozilla Developer network [preferred] and W3Schools)
  • Typing profiency (or practice if needed)

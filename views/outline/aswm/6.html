jsonp vs xhr

The fundamental difference between XHR and JSONP can be nutshelled as thus: XHR content must be requested off of your server. JSONP content can be requested from any server. I remember learning about JSONP long after a had learned the ropes of Ajax, and remember feeling awestruck at how obvious it was.

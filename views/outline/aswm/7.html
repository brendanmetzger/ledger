Carousels

Picture a merry go round spinning in front of you. You have a fixed vantage point, but it moves. Every so often, you see the same thing in front of you.

As a design pattern, a carousel can be conceptually the same thing as a slideshow, or powerpoint, or anything that involves a series of views that you engage with one at a time. It will never see a number greater than 12, but the numbers will repeat as time keeps moving forward. Some very old minds have already devised a simple algebra to describe this process.

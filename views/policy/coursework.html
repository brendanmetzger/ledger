Coursework

Coursework will consist of the following components, though pop-quizzes, extra-credit, challenges may be thrown in when necessary. Mandatory attendance is expected and all absences marked regardless of excused or not. Make sure that point explicitly clear: I can and usually will be lenient on practice work, quizzes, and to a lesser degree, projects. In the spirit of equity, should you have a valid reason for an absence, then you can do make up work, but that work obviously requires presence*, which will mean extra work in the form of a presentation that you propose to myself and the class. Requesting this must be timely, no later than one week from the time you missed.

This institution take absence seriously. As such, an absence is non-negotiable: missing class unavoidably affects your grade. If you absolutely must miss, then I may work with you to lessen the pain based on the circumstances. Thatâ€™s a one-time only deal.

Description Studies are designed to get you work on specific technical skills while focusing on a design outcome. The skills gained and efforts made in studies should make their way immediately into semester-long project. Quick to start, slow to finish. The project is a semester-long effort; it should reflect some interest of yours and you should be ambitious about what you plan on achieving. Plan to work on projects every week throughout the semester. Quizzes will be low-stress (hopefully) and no cramming necessary. Simply, an opportunity to show you are up to date with the assigned readings. The first quiz will be a mix of fill-in-the-blank, short-answer, and multiple choice. The second will be an in-class essay where you will reflect on the class and address a topic given that day. Participation is obviously showing up and paying attention, but also may include in-class work, pop-quizzes, etc. Courtesy and attention to your peers is paramount. If you have a habit of logging in to your computer and zoning out, you and I will be chatting frequently to address that!

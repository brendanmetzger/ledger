Coursework

Coursework will consist of the following components, though pop-quizzes, extra-credit, challenges may be thrown in when necessary. Mandatory attendance is expected and all abscences marked regardless of excused or not. *In the spirit of equity, should you have a valid reason for an abscence, then you can do make up work, but that work obviously requires presence.

Task Practice Projects Quizzes Participation
Description Practic Assignments will be fairl straight forward. I will compose them to offer a guided opportunity to practice. They may be challenging at points, but that is a good thing: see participation Projects are personal, and ambition, creativity and effort are extremely important. Quizzes will be low-stress (hopefully) and no cramming necessary. Simply, an opportunity to show you are up to date with the assigned readings. They will be difficult, a mix of fill-in-the-blank, short-answer, and multiple choice. They will be graded on a curve so due-dilegence will be rewarded. Participation is obviously showing up and paying attention, submitting the in-class work, etc., but, some of this is difficult to measure, so you will need to ask questions via the question mechanism. You will lose -10% per abscense, up to one absence can be made up*.

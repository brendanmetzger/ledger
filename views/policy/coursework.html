Coursework and Grading Policies

Because of the amount of material we are going to cover, this course will be somewhat challenging and we are going to go at a brisk pace. Making mistakes and misunderstanding the material will not affect your grade; paying attention, showing improvement week-by-week, and making the effort to engage with the material should be your primary concern. When the class rises to the challenge, this benefits everyone.

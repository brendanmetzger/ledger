Frequently Asked Questions

Are there instances where px is preferred to some other unit?
Pixels can be useful when you only need about one of them: ie, a border or outline, a hairline margin between two elements. If not a hairline, then no, anything more than 2px is probably unnecessary.
Why is my text breaking outside of my container element?
Likely, because you have a physical height set, and once you tell an element how tall it can (or cannot) be, some of its content is liable to spill out if necessary. This is something that can be used to your advantage.
Overflow
Why are the margins of two elements next to each other not as big as they should be?
Because of margins collapse.
What is the difference between block-level and inline Elements?
Block-level Elements define their own space
Inline Elements can wrap and flow according to the size limits of the parent container.

Case Study: Censoring Keyboards

In Turkey, a law dating back to the 1920s (ended in 2013) banned the use of the letters Q, W and X. This is merely an exercise, and perhaps a decent lesson in the ways that those in power (you the interactive designer) can stifle the opportunities of the citizenry (the user) with some insensitive design implementations. Ultimately, that resides in the field of user experience and accessibility design, so focusing on the how is Ok as the only applicable take-away skill.

Command Keys

We now are familiar with using command+arrow keys to jump around our editor. We know that holding shift down while doing this will select text. If you are not aware of any of this stuff yet, or need a refresher: re-watch the lectures or ask me at any point if you need to clarify—this is the convenient stuff that will make your life easier!

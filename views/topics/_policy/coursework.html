Coursework and Grading Policies

Because of the large amount of material I plan to cover, we will go at a brisk pace. This may present a challenge to some but it is not impossible. In fact, making mistakes and misunderstanding the material will not present an issue with me; paying attention, showing improvement week-by-week, and making the effort to engage with the material should be your primary concern. I will teach you that you do not have to work hard to go far, you must learn to work efficiently. If you and your classmates rise to this challenge, it will benefit everyone.
